Paced Breathing

Minimum: 1 minute

Start Exercise?

00 Hr 00 Min 00 Sec 00
Background

Slowing down our breathing and making our exhale longer than our inhale activates the parasympathetic nervous system, helping regulate our emotions. We always have access to our breath, making paced breathing a very accessible option.

Instructions

Place one hand on your abdomen and notice the rise and fall with each deep breath. Breath in as the circle expands, and breath out as the circle contracts. For some, longer exhales may be uncomfortable and require more practice. Just slowing and deepening your breath can have some effect.
